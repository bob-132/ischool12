Top Attractions in Egypt

Discover the top tourist attractions in Egypt.

The Pyramids of Giza

The Pyramids of Giza are ancient pyramids located in the Giza Plateau. They are one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World and a testament to ancient Egyptian civilization. Visitors can explore the Great Pyramid, the Pyramid of Khafre, and the Pyramid of Menkaure.

Khan Al Khalili

Khan Al Khalili is a famous bazaar and souq in the historic center of Cairo. It is known for its vibrant atmosphere, traditional shops, and cafes. Visitors can find a variety of goods, from spices and jewelry to textiles and souvenirs, making it a must-visit spot for tourists.
